Introduction

From a young age I’ve always had a great appreciation for visual beauty. I was always a strongly visual person who tended to stop and gaze at the hidden beauty of ordinary life. Anything and everything from the stranger on the street, to the birds in the air, to the green of summer trees, holds a special and unique beauty which can evoke emotion. Although I never had access to much photography equipment or many artistic utilities growing up, I always found myself fixating on the visual and trying to capture the emotions it can carry. When I eventually did get my hands on a camera a few years ago, I found myself lost in a passion to capture the momentary feelings which visual images have always brought me.

Content

Hello Cupcake

A lonely little birthday cupcake

This shot was alot of fun to create, and not just because I got cupcakes. Birthdays tend to have a variety of meanings to everyone. Some consider the passing of another year to be another sign of their life fading slowly, while others consider it a celebration of themselves. I felt the mixture of emotions and variety of views was best represented in simple terms.

Camera Settings

  • ISO 1600
  • f/10
